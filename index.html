Meet Me

Who I Am

I’m a graphic and web designer from Long Island, NY who loves art, movies, a good craft beer, and the occasional magic trick. I work as a designer for Respage, a digital marketing solution for the Multi-family industry.

Since the first grade I’ve been in love with art. From the masters of the Renaissance to the contemporary artists of today, I enjoy art in all it’s forms and appearances. Web design came by way of interacting online with friends and artists. As a result, I’ve shifted from the canvas to the screen. For the past 11 years I have worked with 4Walls and Respage to develop our online presence, as well as create websites for our clients.

I’m excited to expand my skills with Thinkful - and look forward to diving into JavaScript and Jquery.

Where I'm From

I’m from Long Island, NY. I like being out in the burbs, but still close enough to Manhattan - only a short drive away!

What Am I Doing Here?

I have come to Thinkful looking to better myself as a designer.

I’m looking to expand my skills, as well as address and correct any bad habits I’ve picked up along the way in my web design career. I’m interested in mastering semantic HTML, as well as developing my skills with JavaScript and jquery - eventually moving into LESS and even some php.

What I Do

Website Design

Do you need a new website? Does your existing website need a facelift? I can help! Using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and jQuery I can transform your artistic vision into a fully-functional, interactive website.

Design Services

Do you need a full-service solution for digital and print media? I'm here from start to finish! With experience in digital and print design, I can offer services across all platforms, delivering a full marketing package.
